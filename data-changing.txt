LF1 Data Changing Reference (from lf-empire.de/lf1-empire/data-changing)

== DATA.DAT Structure ==

Head: character face index (1-11)
Face: face color pair
Skn: skin color pair
Sht: shirt color pair
Trs: trouser/pants color pair
Sp: special move definition

== Special Move Line Format ==
Sp: [combo] [frame1] [frame2] [frame3] [frame4] [projectile_type] [mp_cost]

The LAST number is the MP cost.

== Special Move Combo Keys (all end with Attack) ==
A : left right right Attack
B : right down up Attack
C : right left right Attack (original special)
D : Jump right right Attack
E : up down left right Attack
F : character comes with special weapon, press Attack to use
G : with special weapon press Attack for beam
H : right left right Attack (summon weapon)

== Key Observations ==
- MP costs range from 12 to 200
- John's chargeup (E special) at 200 MP is the most expensive
- Each character has exactly 3 special moves in DATA.DAT
- The cheapest specials (balls) cost 25 MP
- Combo/physical specials cost 100-175 MP
